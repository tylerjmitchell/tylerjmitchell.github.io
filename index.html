About

I am a Ph.D. candidate working with Dr. Paul Knapp in the Carolina Tree-Ring Science Laboratory at UNC Greensboro. I am particularly interested in precipitation–growth responses of pine species at various spatial and temporal scales and in my work I use tree-ring data to analyze historic variability in climate and weather.

A complete list of publications is available by visiting either my Google Scholar or ORCID profile. A current CV is available here.
