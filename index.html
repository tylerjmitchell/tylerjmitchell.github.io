about

Hello! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Wyoming working on forest landscape modeling in the Rocky Mountain region of the United States. I am primarily interested in forests and climate!

Previously, I worked at Esri supporting ArcGIS Online and before that I worked at UNC Greensboro studying how conifers grow in response to specific types and intensities of rainfall.

Outside of work, I enjoy running, reading, walking, music, and being with friends!
